Shipping Policy

Processing times, rates and delivery information

Effective Date: January 1, 2024  ·  Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Mirror Esthetics ships to addresses within the United States. Please review our shipping information below.

1. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days (Monday–Saturday, excluding holidays). You will receive an email confirmation with tracking information once your order ships.

2. Shipping Options & Rates

  • Standard Shipping (5–7 business days) — $8.99 flat rate; FREE on orders over $150
  • Expedited Shipping (2–3 business days) — $18.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day) — $35.00 (order must be placed before 12pm EST)

3. Delivery Estimates

Delivery estimates begin once your order has shipped, not when it is placed. During peak seasons (holidays, promotional events), processing times may be extended by 1–2 business days.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package directly on the carrier's website. If you have not received tracking information within 5 business days of ordering, please contact us.

5. Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate at checkout. Mirror Esthetics is not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer. If you need to update your address, contact us immediately at Mirrorestheticsg1@gmail.com — we can only make changes before the order ships.

6. Lost or Stolen Packages

Once a package is marked as delivered by the carrier, Mirror Esthetics is not responsible for lost or stolen packages. Please contact your local carrier or file a claim with them directly. We recommend selecting shipping with signature confirmation for high-value orders.

7. Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged, please take photos and contact us within 48 hours at Mirrorestheticsg1@gmail.com. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and arrange a replacement if necessary.

8. Affirm & Financing Orders

Orders financed through Affirm follow the same shipping policies as all other orders. Affirm financing approval does not affect shipping timelines.

9. In-Store Pickup

In-store pickup is available at our Gainesville location:
6419 W Newberry Rd, Gainesville, FL 32605
Hours: Monday–Saturday, 9am–6pm. Select "In-Store Pickup" at checkout and we will notify you when your order is ready.
